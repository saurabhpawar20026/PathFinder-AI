"PathFinder AI is an intelligent skill-gap analysis tool designed to help students align their resumes with modern industry standards. Using Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Cosine Similarity, the application compares a user's resume against specific Job Descriptions (JD) to calculate a match score. It identifies missing keywords and generates a personalized 30-day learning roadmap to bridge the identified skill gaps, making students more 'job-ready' for placements
